Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept for designs?

We accept JPG, PNG, PDF, AI (Adobe Illustrator), PSD (Adobe Photoshop), and SVG files. For best results, we recommend vector files (AI, SVG, PDF) or high-resolution images (300 DPI).

Do you provide design services?

Yes! If you don't have a design, we can help create one for you. Just let us know your ideas and we'll work with you to bring your vision to life.

What's the minimum order quantity?

Minimum orders vary by product: Shirts (12 pieces), Hoodies (6 pieces), Hats (12 pieces), Business Cards (100 pieces). Banners have no minimum. Contact us for custom requirements.

Do you offer rush orders?

Yes, we offer rush orders with 1-2 business day turnaround. Rush orders may include additional fees. Contact us to check availability for your specific timeline.

Can I see a proof before printing?

Absolutely! We provide digital proofs for approval before production begins. We want to ensure you're completely satisfied with the design before we print.

Do you ship nationwide?

Yes, we ship nationwide! We also offer local pickup at our Elgin location and contactless delivery for local customers.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, PayPal, Venmo, and Zelle. Payment is typically required before production begins, with payment plans available for large orders.
