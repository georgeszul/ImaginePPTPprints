Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept for designs?

We accept AI, EPS, PNG, and JPG files. For best quality, we recommend vector files (AI, EPS) or high-resolution PNG files (300 DPI or higher).

How long does it take to complete an order?

Standard orders take 3-7 business days. Rush orders are available for an additional fee. Contact us for specific timeline requirements.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer volume discounts for orders of 25+ pieces. The more you order, the better the price per item.

What materials do you use?

We use premium cotton, cotton-polyester blends, and high-quality fabrics. All materials are carefully selected for comfort and durability.

Can you help with design if I don't have artwork?

Absolutely! We offer free design consultation and can help create or modify designs to meet your needs.

What sizes do you offer?

We offer sizes from XS to 5XL for most products. Custom sizing is available for special orders.
